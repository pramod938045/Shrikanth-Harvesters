About Us

Shrikanth Harvesters is a trusted agricultural service provider dedicated to helping farmers harvest crops efficiently and on time. With modern harvesting machines and experienced operators, we provide reliable harvesting services across Karnataka.


Our goal is to support farmers by reducing labour work, saving time, and improving crop productivity. We use advanced harvesting machines designed for multiple crops to ensure fast and clean harvesting with minimal grain loss.


Apart from harvesting, we also provide agriculture support services such as land leveling, ploughing, seed sowing, and tractor logistics services.

Crops We Harvest

Maize – ಮೆಕ್ಕೆಜೋಳ

Maize is one of the most widely cultivated cereal crops valued for its high yield and versatility. It grows best in warm climates with well-drained soil and usually matures within 3–4 months. Efficient harvesting is important to minimize grain loss and maintain quality.

Mung Dal – ಹೆಸರುಕಾಳು

Brown gram is a nutritious pulse crop rich in protein and widely grown in warm regions. It has a short growing period of around 2–3 months. Proper harvesting prevents pod shattering and helps farmers maintain high yield.

Chickpea – ಕಡಲೆ

Chickpea is an important pulse crop known for its high protein and nutritional value. It grows well in dry and cool climates and is harvested within 3–4 months. Timely harvesting ensures good grain quality.

Toor Dal – ತೊಗರಿ

Pigeon pea is a major pulse crop grown across India. It is rich in protein and nutrients and usually takes 5–6 months to mature. Efficient harvesting reduces pod loss and increases productivity.
